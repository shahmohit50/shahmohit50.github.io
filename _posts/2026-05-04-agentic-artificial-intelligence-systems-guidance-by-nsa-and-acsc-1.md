# Agentic Artificial Intelligence Systems guidance by NSA and ACSC — What’s Actually Happening?
The recent guidance on Agentic Artificial Intelligence Systems by the NSA and ACSC has sparked intense interest. But beneath the surface, it's not just about new regulations - it's about the accelerating pace of AI integration into critical systems.

The real reason this is trending is that it represents a significant shift in how governments and institutions view AI: from a peripheral tool to a central component of national security and infrastructure. The stakes are higher than ever, with agencies and companies racing to harness AI's potential.

## 🧩 What This Actually Is (No BS Explanation)
Agentic AI refers to systems that can autonomously make decisions, adapting to new situations without explicit human input. This guidance provides a framework for developers to ensure these systems are secure, reliable, and aligned with human values. Think of it as a set of guardrails for the wild west of AI development.
