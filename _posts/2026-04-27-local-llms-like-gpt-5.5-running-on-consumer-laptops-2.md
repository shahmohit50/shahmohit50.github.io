People are excited because local LLMs promise to put AI power back in the users' hands, away from cloud reliance. This sparks debates on data ownership and security.

GPT-5.5 is a large language model that can run on consumer laptops, thanks to optimizations. It's like having a super-smart, text-based assistant that doesn't need the internet.

Companies like OpenAI are pushing the boundaries of what's possible with local AI. However, the real challenge is not just tech, but convincing users to care about data ownership.
